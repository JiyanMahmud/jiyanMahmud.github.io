About

I work on explainable and unsupervised anomaly detection, with a focus on evaluation frameworks, graph-based root cause attribution, and user-facing explainability systems.

My current research directions include graph-based anomaly propagation, language-based anomaly reasoning and log modeling, and real-time XAI dashboards for operational decision support.

I collaborate on multiple industry-funded projects with Deutsche Telekom Innovation Laboratories in partnership with ELTE.
